"Inception" is a mind-bending science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores the story of Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is given a chance to have his past crimes forgiven if he can successfully perform inception: planting an idea into a target's subconscious.
